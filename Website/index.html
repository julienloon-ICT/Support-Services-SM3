About This Environment

Blue-Green Deployment is a release management strategy that minimizes downtime and risk by running two environments, Blue and Green. The Blue environment is your current production version, while the Green environment is used for staging or updates. Once tested, the Green environment becomes live with minimal disruption. This strategy is ideal for applications with high availability requirements.
